Task-3: Core KPIs Definition
1. Total Revenue
Formula: Sum of total_purchase_amount
Business Importance: Measures overall business performance and sales volume.

2. Average Customer Age
Formula: Average of age
Business Importance: Helps understand customer demographics for targeted marketing.

3. Average Purchase Value
Formula: Total Revenue / Number of Customers
Business Importance: Indicates customer spending behavior.

4. Customer Retention Indicator
Formula: Customers with repeat purchases / Total customers
Business Importance: Helps analyze customer loyalty.

5. Top Revenue Country
Formula: Country with highest total_purchase_amount
Business Importance: Identifies strongest market region.
